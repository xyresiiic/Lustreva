📦 Track Your Order

Stay updated on your delivery

Track Order

To track your order, please sign in to your account and visit the My Orders section in your profile. You'll find real-time status updates for all your orders.

Order Statuses

  • Pending — Your order has been received and is being processed.
  • Confirmed — Your order has been confirmed and is being prepared.
  • Shipped — Your order is on its way! You will receive tracking details via email.
  • Delivered — Your order has been delivered successfully.

For any queries about your order, please contact our support team.

↩ Returns Policy

Hassle-free returns within 7 days

Returns & Refund Policy

At Lustréva, your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, we're here to help.

Return Window

You may request a return within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition as received.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Personalised or custom-engraved products
  • Food and perishable items (chocolates, dry fruits, etc.)
  • Gift cards and vouchers
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"

How to Initiate a Return

  • Email us at returns@lustreva.in with your Order ID
  • Our team will arrange a pickup within 2–3 business days
  • Refund will be processed within 5–7 business days after inspection

Refund Method

Refunds are credited to the original payment method. For COD orders, we will initiate a bank transfer.

✦ About Lustréva

Our story of thoughtful gifting

Our Story

Lustréva was born from a simple idea: gifting should be effortless, elegant, and meaningful. Founded in 2023, we set out to create India's most thoughtful gifting platform — one that combines artisan craftsmanship with modern convenience.

Our Mission

To transform every occasion into a memorable experience through curated, premium gifts that speak louder than words. We believe the right gift, presented beautifully, can strengthen any relationship.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Curated Collections — Every product is handpicked by our gifting experts
  • Artisan Quality — We partner with local artisans and premium brands
  • Stunning Packaging — Unboxing is part of the experience
  • Pan-India Delivery — We deliver to every corner of India
  • Purpose-Driven — A portion of every sale supports NGO partners

The Team

We're a passionate team of designers, curators, and gifting enthusiasts based in Bangalore. We obsess over every detail — from the ribbon on the box to the smile on the recipient's face.

How we protect your data

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Information We Collect

When you use Lustréva, we collect information you provide directly: name, email address, shipping address, and payment details. We also collect usage data such as pages visited and products viewed.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To send order confirmation and shipping updates
  • To improve our products and website experience

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption and secure payment processing. We never store your full payment card details.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with logistics partners (for delivery) and payment processors (for transactions).
